<h2>RVMUN</h2>
<p>RVMUN prides itself on youth empowerment by equipping them with knowledge
and skills in order to understand and address the intricacies and complexities
of the world of current affairs and international relations. RVMUN aims
to provide delegates with the essential and critical insight into global
issues, and foster a sense of responsibility and integrity in addressing
and finding solutions to these challenges.</p>
