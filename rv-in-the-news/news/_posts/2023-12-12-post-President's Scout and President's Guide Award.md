<p>Lee Weng Kee Harry and Toh Quan Xiu Javelle received the prestigious President's
Scout Award and President's Guide Award respectively at the Istana last
month from President Tharman Shanmugaratnam. These are the highest awards
conferred on youth members of the Singapore Scout Association and the highest
level of achievement for a Guide. Their outstanding leadership, good character,
and commitment to serve with empathy have left a heartfelt mark on the
RV and local community.</p>
