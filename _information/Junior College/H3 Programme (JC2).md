<h2>H3 Programme (JC2)</h2>
<h4>BACKGROUND</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The 2006 ‘A’ level curriculum introduced a set of programmes at Higher
3 (H3) level to cater to the learning interests of the top 10% to 15% of
the ‘A’ level cohort who have exceptional interest and aptitude in a particular
subject. The intention is to provide them with opportunities to pursue
their passions and stretch themselves.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The H3 programme builds on the knowledge and skills taught at Higher 2
(H2) level but in a more specialised area. Students who wish to offer a
programme at H3 level would have to offer the corresponding subject at
H2 level. With this approach, students would acquire the foundational knowledge
needed for an H3 programme.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>MOE-CAMBRIDGE AND MOE PARTNER H3 PROGRAMMES</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Table 1 below shows the MOE-CAMBRIDGE and MOE Partner H3 programmes in
River Valley High School that are available for student application.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The MOE-CAMBRIDGE H3 programme provides students with diverse learning
opportunities for in-depth study. The syllabus of a H3 subject offered
at 'A' level can be found online at the following URL: <a href="https://www.seab.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.seab.gov.sg</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The H3 programmes offered by MOE Partners (such as NUS, NTU and SMU) may
take the modes of an extended research essay or a university-taught course.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h4>HIGHER 3 GRADING SYSTEM</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The candidates’ grades for MOE-Cambridge and MOE Partner H3 programme
will be reflected in the MOE-issued School Graduation Certificate and Singapore-Cambridge
GCE A-level Certificate.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The H3 gradings are Distinction, Merit, Pass and Ungraded.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p>Withdrawal of&nbsp; students after the start of instruction:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Students who withdraw&nbsp;within&nbsp;the withdrawal period will have
to pay the&nbsp;$60 course fee&nbsp;to NUS/NTU/SMU.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who withdraw&nbsp;&nbsp;after&nbsp;the withdrawal period will
have the following penalties imposed on top of the $60 course fee:</p>
</li>
<li>

fee to SEAB and international students will have to pay a $70 administrative
fee to SEAB.</p>

for the H3 subject.</p>
