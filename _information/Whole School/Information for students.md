<p><u>Morning Assembly</u> 
</p>
<p>Morning Assembly starts at 9 am on Monday and 8 am from Tues-Fri. Students
must be at the assembly area or classrooms punctually at 9 am on Monday
and 8 am (Tues-Fri) or they will be considered late for school.</p>
<p><u>School Uniform</u>
</p>
<p>Students must wear their school uniform with respect and pride. Students
can refer to the school handbook for more information.</p>
<p><u>Safety Measures</u>
</p>
<p>The safety of our students is our top priority. We kindly request your
cooperation with the following:</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Drop-off locations for vehicles:</p>
<p>Students must be dropped off only at these two designated points:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Covered foyer at the roundabout</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Designated point along the school access road</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Please do not drop off along
Boon Lay Avenue or any other location as this poses a traffic hazard.</p>
<p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; School entry points:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Main gate (Gate 1) along Boon Lay Avenue &amp; Gate 3 along Boon Lay Drive</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; School exit points:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Main Gate (Gate 1) along Boon Lay Avenue &amp; Gate 4 along Boon Lay Drive</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>5.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To ensure a smooth traffic
flow, please do not wait at the drop-off points.</p>
