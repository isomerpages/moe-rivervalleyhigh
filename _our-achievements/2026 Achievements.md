<p>Scientific Publication in Avian Research</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Teo Shen Han (24J02) was recently featured in <a href="https://www.zaobao.com.sg/news/singapore/story20260310-8687036" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Lianhe Zaobao</a> on
10 March 2026 for his outstanding research journey in biodiversity and
wildlife conservation. Driven by a natural curiosity about animals, Shen
Han conducted research on bird taxonomy under the MOE Science Research
Programme. His project earned a Gold award at the Singapore Science and
Engineering Fair in March 2025 and led to the rare distinction of publishing
and first-authoring a <a href="https://www.j-avianres.com/article/doi/10.1016/j.avrs.2025.100307" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">scientific research article</a> in
the international journal <em>Avian Research</em> in December 2025 while
he was in JC2.</p>
<p>Shen Han's <a href="https://www.rivervalleyhigh.moe.edu.sg/2025-gce-a-level-results/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">journey</a>—from
3D food printing with cricket proteins at SUTD to avian evolution research
at NUS—exemplifies how RV creates opportunities for students to pursue
their passions through diverse programmes. This holistic approach enables
students to transform curiosity into meaningful contributions to our community
and society, becoming changemakers ready to tackle global challenges.</p>
